## Overview

Domain shift is a fundamental challenge in deploying time series models across different operating conditions. This project develops **novel contrastive learning approaches** for domain adaptation that align representations across domains while preserving temporal structure.

## Key Contributions

- **Source-Free Domain Adaptation with Temporal Imputation** -- MAPU introduces a source-free approach that adapts models without access to source data, using temporal imputation to handle missing temporal patterns (KDD 2023).
- **Self-Supervised Autoregressive Domain Adaptation** -- SLARDA combines self-supervised learning with autoregressive modeling for robust domain adaptation in time series (IEEE TNNLS 2022).
- **Contrastive Adversarial Domain Adaptation** -- CADA leverages contrastive and adversarial training for cross-domain RUL prediction of machinery (IEEE TII 2020).
