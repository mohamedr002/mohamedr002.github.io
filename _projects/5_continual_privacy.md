## Overview

Real-world systems evolve over time, and data privacy is increasingly critical. This project develops **continual learning and privacy-preserving AI** methods that adapt to changing conditions while protecting sensitive information.

## Key Contributions

- **EverAdapt: Continuous Adaptation for Dynamic Fault Diagnosis** -- A framework for continuous test-time adaptation that handles evolving machine conditions without forgetting previously learned knowledge (MSSP 2025).
- **Privacy-Preserving Domain Adaptation** -- Methods that enable cross-domain knowledge transfer for time series while maintaining strict data privacy guarantees.
- **Federated Transfer Learning for Fault Diagnosis** -- Combining federated learning with transfer learning to enable collaborative fault diagnosis across distributed industrial sites without sharing raw data (FL-AAAI 2022).
- **Continual Test-Time Adaptation** -- Approaches for adapting deployed models to distribution shifts at inference time, enabling robust performance in non-stationary environments.

## Selected Publications

- **EverAdapt** -- Continuous Adaptation for Dynamic Machine Fault Diagnosis, *MSSP 2025*
- **Federated Transfer Learning** -- Privacy-Preserving Fault Diagnosis, *FL-AAAI 2022*

## Research Goals

- Build AI systems that **adapt continuously** without catastrophic forgetting
- Enable **collaborative learning** across organizations without compromising data privacy
- Develop **robust deployment strategies** for models operating in non-stationary environments
